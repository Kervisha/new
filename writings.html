Medium-sized Metaphors

She is not a silent thunder.
She is not the calm after the storm.
She is not peace made plentiful.
Neither passive nor passive aggressive.
She cannot be likened to the soothing flow of a calm stream.
She is not a still breeze on a cool evening.
She is lightening.
She is called Arson.
She is an abundance of emotions worn for all to see.
She is a cold, raging waterfall.
She is not the perfect blend of sweet and salty.
She is fire and ice.
